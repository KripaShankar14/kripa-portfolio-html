Best Movies according to Kripashankar

Top 3 Movies of all Time

Transformers: Rise Of The Beast

Optimus Prime and the Autobots take on their biggest challenge yet.
When a new threat capable of destroying the entire planet emerges,
they must team up with a powerful faction of Transformers known as
the Maximals to save earth .

John Wick: Chapter 4

With the price on his head ever increasing, legendary hit man John Wick
takes his fight against the High Table global as he seeks out the most
powerful players in the underworld, from New York to Paris to Japan to Berlin.

Thor: Love and Thunder

Thor embarks on a journey unlike anything he's ever faced -- a quest for inner peace.
However, his retirement gets interrupted by Gorr the God Butcher, a galactic killer
who seeks the extinction of the gods. To combat the threat, Thor enlists the help of King Valkyrie,
Korg and ex-girlfriend Jane Foster, who -- to his surprise -- inexplicably wields his magical hammer.
Together, they set out on a harrowing cosmic adventure to uncover the mystery of the
God Butcher's vengeance.
